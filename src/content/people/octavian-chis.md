His through-line is his own motto, building tools that make people happy. At Indie TM #11 he showed Intersect, an intent graph for the indie scene: every builder files a handful of cards for what they are working on, what they need, what they can offer, what they are questioning, what they are thinking about, and what they have discovered, and the system reads those cards to generate each person's profile and surface the intersections between people, most usefully a need on one side met by an offer on the other."
